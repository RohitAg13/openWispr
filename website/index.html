Questions

Good questions, honest answers.

Does it really work offline?+
Yes — completely. The speech and polish models live on your device. Once installed, OpenWispr never needs a network connection to transcribe, format, or refine your text.
Is it actually free?+
Yes. OpenWispr is free and MIT-licensed. There’s no paid tier, no usage cap, and no account. The models run on your hardware, so there are no server costs to pass on to you.
Will the models slow down my machine?+
OpenWispr ships right-sized models tuned for on-device speed. Transcription is near real-time on modern Macs and Android devices, and you can pick a lighter model if you prefer.
Which languages are supported?+
The underlying Whisper-class models support dozens of languages out of the box. You select your primary language in Settings, and dictation works fully offline in each.
Can I delete my history?+
Anytime. History is stored locally and you can clear individual entries or wipe everything in one click. You can also turn history off entirely so nothing is ever written to disk.
What platforms are supported?+
macOS and Android are available today from GitHub Releases. Windows and Linux builds are in active development — star the repo to follow along.
